What is get active.

A simple daily log that shows you
exactly where you stand.

01
Thirty seconds. Then you're done.
Open the app. Pick your activity — a walk, a gym session, a backyard cricket game, anything. Save. That's it. No lengthy check-ins. No complicated flows. The app gets out of your way so you can get on with your day.
02
Your honest number. Always.
No inflated streaks. No confetti for average days. Just your active days this year, your pace, and where you need to be. One bad week doesn't reset anything. Rest days are logged too — Sick, Holiday, Rest all have a colour.
03
Built around a year, not a day.
Set your goal — 75% of days active, 60%, whatever fits your life. The app tracks it across the full year. Logging every day builds something. Not just fitness. An identity. The kind of person who shows up.
