Get Moving.

Whether you're just starting out and trying to build the habit, or you've been active for years and want to stay consistent — get active. works for both. You set your own goal. You define what active means. A walk counts. A bike ride counts. A backyard cricket game counts. The app just wants to know: did you move today?

"You don't need to be an athlete to use this.
You just need to want to be someone who moves."
